What is Lyrically?

Lyrically is a website designed for you to find new songs that contain a specific lyric. Simply type your lyric into the search bar, press the search icon, and discover multiple songs with that lyric. Once you see the results, click on one to learn more about that song and see its lyrics. Lyrically was built as part of the full-stack web developement course at Portland State University by the students Alyssa Vy, Mike Wilson, and Kenzie Gage.
